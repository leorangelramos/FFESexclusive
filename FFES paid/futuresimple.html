Teoria e Contexto em Ação: Futuro Simples (WILL)

Veja em ação:
John: "The phone is ringing!"
Ana: "OK, I will answer it." (Decisão no momento da fala)

Usamos o Futuro Simples com WILL para decisões espontâneas, previsões, promessas ou ofertas.

A fórmula é: Sujeito + WILL + Verbo na Forma Base.

  • O WILL é usado para *todas* as pessoas (I, You, He, She, It, We, They).
  • O verbo principal *nunca* muda (sem '-s', sem '-ing').
  • A negativa é WILL NOT (contração: WON'T).

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Para I / You / We / They:

  • Afirmativa: I will travel.
  • Negativa: I will not (won't) travel.
  • Interrogativa: Will I travel?

Para He / She / It:

  • Afirmativa: She will work.
  • Negativa: She will not (won't) work.
  • Interrogativa: Will she work?

Escolha a forma correta (Afirmativa, Negativa e Interrogativa):
1. "Eu comerei."

a) I will eat / I will not eat / Will I eat?
b) I will eats / I will not eats / Will I eats?

2. "Ela trabalhará."

a) She will works / She will not works / Will she works?
b) She will work / She will not work / Will she work?

3. "Eles jogarão."

a) They will play / They will not play / Will they play?
b) They will playing / They will not playing / Will they playing?

4. "Ele assistirá TV."

a) He will watches TV / He will not watches TV / Will he watches TV?
b) He will watch TV / He will not watch TV / Will he watch TV?

5. "Nós estudaremos."

a) We will study / We will not study / Will we study?
b) We study / We not study / Do we study?

6. "Você lerá."

a) You will to read / You will not to read / Will you to read?
b) You will read / You will not read / Will you read?

7. "O gato dormirá."

a) The cat will sleep / It will not sleep / Will it sleep?
b) The cat will sleeps / It will not sleeps / Will it sleeps?

8. "Eu dirigirei."

a) I drive / I not drive / Will I driving?
b) I will drive / I will not drive / Will I drive?

9. "Ela cozinhará."

a) She will cook / She will not cook / Will she cook?
b) She will cooks / She will not cooks / Will she cooks?

10. "Eles correrão."

a) They running / They not run / Are they run?
b) They will run / They will not run / Will they run?
