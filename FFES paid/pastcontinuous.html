Teoria e Contexto em Ação: Passado Contínuo (WAS / WERE)

Veja em ação:
John: "What were you doing when I called?"
Ana: "I was working on the report. He was helping me."

Usamos o Passado Contínuo para falar de ações que estavam *em progresso* em um momento específico do passado (ex: "ontem à noite") ou que foram interrompidas por outra ação.

A fórmula é: Sujeito + Verbo to Be no Passado (WAS/WERE) + Verbo com -ING.

  • WAS: Usado com I, He, She, It. (I was working)
  • WERE: Usado com You, We, They. (They were working)

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Para I / He / She / It:

  • Afirmativa: I was eating.
  • Negativa: I was not (wasn't) eating.
  • Interrogativa: Was I eating?
  • Afirmativa: He was sleeping.
  • Negativa: He was not sleeping.
  • Interrogativa: Was he sleeping?

Para You / We / They:

  • Afirmativa: They were studying.
  • Negativa: They were not (weren't) studying.
  • Interrogativa: Were they studying?

Escolha a forma correta (Afirmativa, Negativa e Interrogativa):
1. "Eu estava comendo."

a) I was eating / I was not eating / Was I eating?
b) I were eating / I were not eating / Were I eating?

2. "Ela estava trabalhando."

a) She were working / She were not working / Were she working?
b) She was working / She was not working / Was she working?

3. "Eles estavam jogando."

a) They were playing / They were not playing / Were they playing?
b) They was playing / They was not playing / Was they playing?

4. "Ele estava assistindo TV."

a) He were watching TV / He were not watching TV / Were he watching TV?
b) He was watching TV / He was not watching TV / Was he watching TV?

5. "Nós estávamos estudando."

a) We were studying / We were not studying / Were we studying?
b) We was studying / We was not studying / Was we studying?

6. "Você estava lendo."

a) You was reading / You was not reading / Was you reading?
b) You were reading / You were not reading / Were you reading?

7. "O gato estava dormindo."

a) The cat was sleeping / It was not sleeping / Was it sleeping?
b) The cat were sleeping / It were not sleeping / Were it sleeping?

8. "Eu estava dirigindo."

a) I were driving / I were not driving / Were I driving?
b) I was driving / I was not driving / Was I driving?

9. "Ela estava cozinhando."

a) She was cooking / She was not cooking / Was she cooking?
b) She were cooking / She were not cooking / Were she cooking?

10. "Eles estavam correndo."

a) They was running / They was not running / Was they running?
b) They were running / They were not running / Were they running?
