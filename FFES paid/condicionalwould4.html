Teoria: Condicional Perfeito Contínuo (WOULD + HAVE + BEEN + ING)

Veja em ação:
John: "I'm so tired." (Estou tão cansado.)
Ana: "If the meeting hadn't been canceled, we would have been working all night." (Se a reunião não tivesse sido cancelada, nós teríamos estado trabalhando a noite toda.)

Usamos o Condicional Perfeito Contínuo para focar na duração de uma ação hipotética que *teria acontecido no passado* (mas não aconteceu).

A fórmula é: Sujeito + WOULD + HAVE + BEEN + Verbo com -ING.

  • O WOULD HAVE BEEN (e WOULDN'T HAVE BEEN) é usado para *todas* as pessoas.
  • Contrações: 'd have been (Ex: I'd have been) / would've been (Ex: I would've been).

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Para todas as pessoas (I, You, He, She, We, They):

  • Afirmativa: I would (I'd) have been working.
  • Negativa: I would not (wouldn't) have been working.
  • Interrogativa: Would I have been working?
  • Afirmativa: He would (He'd) have been sleeping.
  • Negativa: He would not (wouldn't) have been sleeping.
  • Interrogativa: Would he have been sleeping?

1. "If it hadn't rained, I [_____] working in the garden."

b) would be
c) will have been

2. "She [_____] (teria estado esperando) for hours."

a) would have been waiting
b) would be waiting
c) would have waited

3. "[_____] you have been working there?"

a) Would
b) Will
c) Had

4. "He wouldn't [_____] been living there."

a) have
b) has
c) be

5. "They would have [_____] (estar) sleeping."

a) be
b) been
c) being

6. "We would have been [_____] (dirigir) for ten hours."

a) driving
b) driven
c) drove

7. "It [_____] (não teria estado) working."

a) wouldn't have been
b) wouldn't be
c) hadn't been

8. "I [_____] (teria estado) studying if you hadn't called."

a) would study
b) would have been studying
c) would have studied

9. "She [_____] (não teria estado) crying."

a) wouldn't have been crying
b) wouldn't be crying
c) won't have been crying

10. "[_____] (Ele teria estado) playing?"

a) Will he have been
b) Had he been
c) Would he have been
