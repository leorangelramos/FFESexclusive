Teoria: Passado Perfeito Contínuo (HAD + BEEN + ING)

Veja em ação:
John: "Why were you so tired yesterday?" (Por que você estava tão cansado ontem?)
Ana: "Because I had been running for an hour before I met you." (Porque eu tinha ficado correndo por uma hora antes de te encontrar.)

Usamos o Passado Perfeito Contínuo para focar na duração ou continuidade de uma ação que estava acontecendo *antes* de outra ação no passado.

A fórmula é: Sujeito + HAD + BEEN + Verbo com -ING.

  • O HAD BEEN (e HAD NOT BEEN / HADN'T BEEN) é usado para *todas* as pessoas.
  • Contração: 'd been (Ex: I'd been, She'd been).

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Para todas as pessoas (I, You, He, She, We, They):

  • Afirmativa: I had (I'd) been working.
  • Negativa: I had not (hadn't) been working.
  • Interrogativa: Had I been working?
  • Afirmativa: He had (He'd) been sleeping.
  • Negativa: He had not (hadn't) been sleeping.
  • Interrogativa: Had he been sleeping?

1. "I [_____] waiting for an hour when he arrived."

a) had been
b) have been
c) was

2. "She was tired because she [_____] studying all night."

a) has been
b) had been
c) was

3. "[_____] they been living there long before they moved?"

a) Had
b) Have
c) Did

4. "He hadn't [_____] paying attention."

a) be
b) been
c) being

5. "We had been [_____] (drive) for hours."

a) drove
b) driven
c) driving

6. "It [_____] raining all morning."

a) have been
b) had been
c) was

7. "I [_____] (não tinha dormido) well."

a) hadn't been sleeping
b) wasn't sleeping
c) haven't been sleeping

8. "How long [_____] (você tinha) waiting?"

a) have you been
b) had you been
c) were you

9. "She [_____] (tinha trabalhado) there for 20 years."

a) has been working
b) had been working
c) was working

10. "They had been [_____] (correr)."

a) ran
b) run
c) running
