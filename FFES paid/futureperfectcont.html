Teoria: Futuro Perfeito Contínuo (WILL HAVE BEEN + ING)

Veja em ação:
Ana: "When you retire next year..." (Quando você se aposentar ano que vem...)
John: "Yes, by then, I will have been working here for 30 years!" (Sim, até lá, eu terei estado trabalhando aqui por 30 anos!)

Usamos o Futuro Perfeito Contínuo para focar na duração de uma ação que estará acontecendo *até* um ponto específico no futuro.

A fórmula é: Sujeito + WILL HAVE + BEEN + Verbo com -ING.

  • O WILL HAVE BEEN (e WON'T HAVE BEEN) é usado para *todas* as pessoas.
  • Contração: 'll have been (Ex: I'll have been working, She'll have been working).

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Para todas as pessoas (I, You, He, She, We, They):

  • Afirmativa: I will (I'll) have been working.
  • Negativa: I will not (won't) have been working.
  • Interrogativa: Will I have been working?
  • Afirmativa: He will (He'll) have been sleeping.
  • Negativa: He will not (won't) have been sleeping.
  • Interrogativa: Will he have been sleeping?

Escolha a forma correta:
1. "By 5 PM, I [_____] working for 8 hours."

a) will have been
b) will be
c) have been

2. "Next month, she [_____] living here for a year."

a) will live
b) will have been
c) has been

3. "How long [_____] you have been studying when you graduate?"

a) will
b) have
c) do

4. "He won't [_____] been sleeping long."

a) have
b) has
c) be

5. "By 10 PM, they will have been [_____] (play) games."

a) played
b) playing
c) play

6. "It [_____] (terá estado chovendo) for days."

a) will rain
b) will be raining
c) will have been raining

7. "We [_____] (não teremos estado) waiting long."

a) won't have been
b) won't be
c) haven't been

8. "Will she [_____] (ter estado) teaching for 20 years?"

a) have been
b) has been
c) be

9. "By the time you arrive, I [_____] (terei estado) cooking."

a) will be cooking
b) will have been cooking
c) am cooking

10. "[_____] they have been driving all night?"

a) Will
b) Have
c) Are
