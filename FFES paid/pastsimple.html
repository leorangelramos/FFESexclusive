Teoria e Contexto em Ação: Passado Simples (DID)

Veja em ação:
John: "Hi Ana. Did you work yesterday?"
Ana: "No, I did not work yesterday. I worked this morning."

Usamos o Passado Simples para falar de ações que começaram e terminaram no passado. Para negar e perguntar, usamos o auxiliar DID.

  • Afirmativa: O verbo muda. Se for regular, adicionamos -ED (ex: work -> worked). Se for irregular, ele muda de forma (ex: go -> went).
  • Negativa e Interrogativa: Usamos DID para *todas* as pessoas (I, You, He, She, It, We, They).
  • IMPORTANTE: Quando usamos DID ou DID NOT, o verbo principal volta para a forma base (sem -ed ou sem a forma irregular).

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Verbo Regular (Work):

  • Afirmativa: I worked.
  • Negativa: I did not work. (Verbo 'work' volta ao normal)
  • Interrogativa: Did I work? (Verbo 'work' volta ao normal)
  • Afirmativa: She worked.
  • Negativa: She did not work.
  • Interrogativa: Did she work?

Verbo Irregular (Go):

  • Afirmativa: They went. (Forma irregular)
  • Negativa: They did not go. (Verbo 'go' volta ao normal)
  • Interrogativa: Did they go? (Verbo 'go' volta ao normal)

Escolha a forma correta (Afirmativa, Negativa e Interrogativa):
1. "Eu comi." (Verbo: eat)

a) I ate / I did not eat / Did I eat?
b) I eated / I did not ate / Did I eated?

2. "Ela trabalhou." (Verbo: work)

a) She work / She did not worked / Did she worked?
b) She worked / She did not work / Did she work?

3. "Eles jogaram." (Verbo: play)

a) They played / They did not play / Did they play?
b) They plaied / They did not plaied / Did they plaied?

4. "Ele assistiu TV." (Verbo: watch)

a) He watch / He did not watched / Did he watched?
b) He watched / He did not watch / Did he watch?

5. "Nós estudamos." (Verbo: study)

a) We studied / We did not study / Did we study?
b) We studyed / We did not studyed / Did we studyed?

6. "Você leu." (Verbo: read)

a) You readed / You did not readed / Did you readed?
b) You read / You did not read / Did you read?

7. "O gato dormiu." (Verbo: sleep)

a) The cat slept / It did not sleep / Did it sleep?
b) The cat sleeped / It did not sleeped / Did it sleeped?

8. "Eu dirigi." (Verbo: drive)

a) I drived / I did not drived / Did I drived?
b) I drove / I did not drive / Did I drive?

9. "Ela cozinhou." (Verbo: cook)

a) She cooked / She did not cook / Did she cook?
b) She cook / She did not cooked / Did she cooked?

10. "Eles correram." (Verbo: run)

a) They runned / They did not runned / Did they runned?
b) They ran / They did not run / Did they run?
