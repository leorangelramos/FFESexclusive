Teoria e Contexto em Ação: Passado Perfeito (HAD + PP)

Veja em ação:
John: "Why was Ana so upset?" (Por que a Ana estava tão chateada?)
Mark: "Because she had lost her keys." (Porque ela tinha perdido as chaves dela.)

Usamos o Passado Perfeito para falar sobre o "passado do passado". É uma ação que aconteceu *antes* de outra ação no passado.

A fórmula é: Sujeito + HAD + Verbo no Particípio Passado (PP).

  • O HAD (e HAD NOT / HADN'T) é usado para *todas* as pessoas (I, You, He, She, It, We, They).
  • Contração: 'd (Ex: I'd gone, She'd finished).
  • Particípio Passado (PP): Verbos regulares terminam em -ED (worked). Verbos irregulares têm uma 3ª forma (go -> went -> gone; see -> saw -> seen).

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Verbo Regular (Work):

  • Afirmativa: I had (I'd) worked.
  • Negativa: I had not (hadn't) worked.
  • Interrogativa: Had I worked?

Verbo Irregular (Go):

  • Afirmativa: He had (He'd) gone.
  • Negativa: He had not (hadn't) gone.
  • Interrogativa: Had he gone?

Escolha a forma correta:
1. "I ____ finished when he called."

a) had
b) have
c) was

2. "She [_____] (nunca tinha visto) snow before."

a) had never seen
b) has never seen
c) never saw

3. "____ they left before you arrived?"

a) Had
b) Have
c) Did

4. "He told me he [_____] (tinha esquecido) his wallet."

a) had forgotten
b) has forgotten
c) forgot

5. "The train had already [_____] (partir)."

a) leave
b) leaved
c) left

6. "We [_____] (não tínhamos comido) breakfast."

a) had not eaten
b) have not eaten
c) did not eat

7. "By the time I got there, the movie [_____]."

a) had started
b) started
c) has started

8. "She asked me where I [_____] (tinha estado)."

a) have been
b) had been
c) was

9. "He [_____] (tinha trabalhado) there for 10 years before he quit."

a) had worked
b) has worked
c) worked

10. "They [_____] (tinham comprado) the house."

a) have bought
b) bought
c) had bought
