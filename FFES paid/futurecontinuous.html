Teoria e Contexto em Ação: Futuro Contínuo (WILL BE + ING)

Veja em ação:
John: "Don't call me at 9 PM tonight."
Ana: "Why not?" John: "I will be studying for my exam."

Usamos o Futuro Contínuo para falar de uma ação que estará *em progresso* em um momento específico no futuro.

A fórmula é: Sujeito + WILL BE + Verbo com -ING.

  • A estrutura WILL BE é usada para *todas* as pessoas (I, You, He, She, It, We, They).
  • A negativa é WILL NOT BE (contração: WON'T BE).
  • Na interrogativa, o 'Will' troca de lugar com o sujeito: Will she be working?

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Para I / You / We / They:

  • Afirmativa: I will be traveling.
  • Negativa: I will not be traveling.
  • Interrogativa: Will I be traveling?

Para He / She / It:

  • Afirmativa: She will be working.
  • Negativa: She will not be working.
  • Interrogativa: Will she be working?

Escolha a forma correta (Afirmativa, Negativa e Interrogativa):
1. "Eu estarei comendo."

a) I will be eating / I will not be eating / Will I be eating?
b) I will eating / I will not eating / Will I eating?

2. "Ela estará trabalhando."

a) She will work / She will not work / Will she work?
b) She will be working / She will not be working / Will she be working?

3. "Eles estarão jogando."

a) They will be playing / They will not be playing / Will they be playing?
b) They will be play / They will not be play / Will they be play?

4. "Ele estará assistindo TV."

a) He will watching TV / He will not watching TV / Will he watching TV?
b) He will be watching TV / He will not be watching TV / Will he be watching TV?

5. "Nós estaremos estudando."

a) We will be studying / We will not be studying / Will we be studying?
b) We will study / We will not study / Will we study?

6. "Você estará lendo."

a) You will be read / You will not be read / Will you be read?
b) You will be reading / You will not be reading / Will you be reading?

7. "O gato estará dormindo."

a) The cat will be sleeping / It will not be sleeping / Will it be sleeping?
b) The cat will sleep / It will not sleep / Will it sleep?

8. "Eu estarei dirigindo."

a) I will drive / I will not drive / Will I drive?
b) I will be driving / I will not be driving / Will I be driving?

9. "Ela estará cozinhando."

a) She will be cooking / She will not be cooking / Will she be cooking?
b) She will cook / She will not cook / Will she cook?

10. "Eles estarão correndo."

a) They will run / They will not run / Will they run?
b) They will be running / They will not be running / Will they be running?
