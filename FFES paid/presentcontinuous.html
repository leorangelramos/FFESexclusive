Teoria e Contexto em Ação: Presente Contínuo

Veja em ação:
John: "Hi Ana. Are you working on the report?"
Ana: "Yes, I am working on it now. My boss is waiting."

Usamos o Presente Contínuo para falar de ações acontecendo *agora*, no momento da fala, ou ações temporárias.

A fórmula é: Sujeito + Verbo to Be (AM/IS/ARE) + Verbo com -ING.

  • AM: Usado com I. (I am working)
  • IS: Usado com He, She, It. (She is working)
  • ARE: Usado com You, We, They. (They are working)

Exemplos Práticos da Estrutura:

Para I:

  • Afirmativa: I am eating.
  • Negativa: I am not eating.
  • Interrogativa: Am I eating?

Para You / We / They:

  • Afirmativa: They are studying.
  • Negativa: They are not studying.
  • Interrogativa: Are they studying?

Para He / She / It:

  • Afirmativa: He is sleeping.
  • Negativa: He is not sleeping.
  • Interrogativa: Is he sleeping?

Escolha a forma correta (Afirmativa, Negativa e Interrogativa):
1. "Eu estou comendo."

a) I am eating / I am not eating / Am I eating?
b) I is eating / I is not eating / Is I eating?

2. "Ela está trabalhando."

a) She are working / She are not working / Are she working?
b) She is working / She is not working / Is she working?

3. "Eles estão jogando."

a) They are playing / They are not playing / Are they playing?
b) They is playing / They is not playing / Is they playing?

4. "Ele está assistindo TV."

a) He are watching TV / He are not watching TV / Are he watching TV?
b) He is watching TV / He is not watching TV / Is he watching TV?

5. "Nós estamos estudando."

a) We are studying / We are not studying / Are we studying?
b) We am studying / We am not studying / Am we studying?

6. "Você está lendo."

a) You is reading / You is not reading / Is you reading?
b) You are reading / You are not reading / Are you reading?

7. "O gato está dormindo."

a) The cat is sleeping / It is not sleeping / Is it sleeping?
b) The cat are sleeping / It are not sleeping / Are it sleeping?

8. "Eu estou dirigindo."

a) I are driving / I are not driving / Are I driving?
b) I am driving / I am not driving / Am I driving?

9. "Ela está cozinhando."

a) She is cooking / She is not cooking / Is she cooking?
b) She am cooking / She am not cooking / Am she cooking?

10. "Eles estão correndo."

a) They is running / They is not running / Is they running?
b) They are running / They are not running / Are they running?
